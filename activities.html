Adventure Readiness

Common questions from HR directors and team leads regarding our high-impact programs.

Do we need prior experience?

No. While some activities are "Advanced," our instructors provide full training and safety briefings. We tailor the intensity to your team's comfort level.

What should we wear?

We provide a detailed gear list for every activity. Generally, athletic wear and sturdy footwear are required. We provide all technical gear (harnesses, helmets, etc.).

Is it safe for all ages/abilities?

Safety is our #1 priority. We have a 100% safety record. Many activities have "low-impact" modifications to ensure every team member can participate meaningfully.

How do we handle emergencies?

Every instructor is WFR (Wilderness First Responder) certified. We maintain satellite communication and have evacuation protocols for every remote location.

Need a Custom Blueprint?

We can design an activity specific to your exact corporate metrics and duration.

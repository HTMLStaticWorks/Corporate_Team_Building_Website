Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge per person or per event?

Our core packages are priced per event based on a maximum capacity to ensure proper instructor-to-participant ratios for safety. If you exceed the tier capacity, incremental per-head charges apply.

Is the dashboard software an extra monthly fee?

No. Access to the Elevate Client Dashboard for managing waivers, rosters, and media is complimentarily included for 6 months pre and post-event.
